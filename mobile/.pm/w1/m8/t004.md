## Objective

Make the editor always resolvable to a balanced beancount transaction: a running remainder footer shows how far the legs are from zero, the final leg auto-fills the balancing amount (marked "auto", overridable), and Done stays disabled — with a toast explaining why — until every leg has an account and the amounts sum to exactly zero.

## Context

- All money math in integer cents (helpers from t003's `multi-leg-utils.ts`) — never float sums.
- Single operating currency in v1 (first of `currencies` from `useLedgerMeta`, as quick add does).
- Toast via `useToast` (`src/common/hooks`), mirroring `amountEmptyError` usage in `add-transaction-screen.tsx`.

## Steps

1. `multi-leg-utils.ts`: `remainder(legs)` and `autoFillLast(legs)` pure functions; a leg the user has manually edited stops auto-tracking (tap to re-enable "auto").
2. Footer row under the legs card: localized "Remainder" + amount; visually distinguish balanced (0.00 ✓) from unbalanced using theme tokens.
3. Gate the header Done button: disabled until balanced and all legs have accounts and non-zero amounts; tapping while invalid shows a localized toast naming the problem.

## Files

- `src/screens/add-transaction-screen/multi-leg-screen.tsx`
- `src/screens/add-transaction-screen/multi-leg-utils.ts`

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Editing any non-auto leg updates the auto leg and the remainder immediately; overriding the auto leg surfaces a non-zero remainder.
- [ ] Done is disabled (with explanatory toast on tap) for: unbalanced legs, missing account, zero-amount leg.
- [ ] Balance math is exact for amounts like 0.10 + 0.20 (integer cents, no FP drift).

## Out of scope

- Multi-currency balancing and `@` price annotations.
