## Objective

Make suggestions visible and tappable: under the "to" account row, show the payee's alternative historical accounts as chips; when the payee has no history, call `suggestTransactionCategoriesWithLLM` and show its suggestions as chips instead (marked as AI suggestions). Tapping a chip applies it; the account picker remains the full-control path.

## Context

- Row UI: `ListItem` in `src/screens/add-transaction-screen/` — add an optional chips slot below the "to" row on `add-transaction-next-screen.tsx`.
- Chips: small themed pills (theme tokens; skeleton tiles per loading conventions while the LLM call is in flight).
- Distinguish sources: history chips plain, AI chips with a subtle marker (e.g. sparkle icon) — the user should know when AI is proposing.

## Steps

1. Chips row component (skeleton state, horizontal scroll for overflow); tapping applies the account and tracks `suggestion_chip_tapped` with `{ source: "history" | "llm" }`.
2. LLM fallback in the t002 hook: fire only when history is empty and payee is non-empty; never auto-apply LLM results — chips only (AI proposes, user confirms).
3. Quota/failure on the LLM call: chips row simply doesn't render; no error interrupts capture.

- [ ] Known payee: runner-up historical accounts render as chips; tap applies.
- [ ] Unknown payee: AI chips render (visibly AI-marked) and are never auto-applied.
- [ ] LLM failure/quota exhaustion renders nothing and blocks nothing.

## Out of scope
