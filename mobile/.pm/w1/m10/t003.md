## Objective

Turn the picked image into a parsed proposal: request a presigned upload URL, PUT the image to S3, call `parseReceiptWithLLM` with the returned `objectKey`, and show clear progress ("uploading… / reading receipt…") plus honest quota and failure states.

## Steps

1. Capture-flow hook (e.g. `use-receipt-capture.ts` under `src/screens/add-transaction-screen/` or a new `src/screens/receipt-capture-screen/`): acquire → upload → parse state machine with cancellation.

3. Analytics: `receipt_parse_started`, `receipt_parse_succeeded` / `receipt_parse_failed`.
