## Objective

The Spending segment shows monthly expense totals as a bar chart with a category breakdown list underneath — each expense category with amount and % of total, top-N with an "other" bucket — Monarch's spending report.

## Context

- Data: `use-income-statement` (t001) — `expensesData` for the bars, `expensesHierarchyData` for the breakdown.
- Chart: `src/common/d3/bar-chart-d3.tsx` (used by `src/screens/home-screen/components/spending-card.tsx` — reuse its month-bucketing approach).
- Balance parsing: `src/screens/home-screen/selectors/balance-util.ts` (multi-currency `JSONObject` balances).
- Category naming: strip the `Expenses:` prefix for display, keep full account names for drill-down (t007).

## Steps

1. Selector `select-spending-report.ts`: monthly totals from `expensesData` for the selected range; flatten `expensesHierarchyData` to top-level categories with amount + share, top-N + "other".
2. Component `spending-report.tsx`: bar chart + breakdown rows (name, amount, %, subtle share bar).
3. Wire the shared time range from t002 into the query `time` variable.
4. Loading skeletons with themed backgrounds; empty state for ledgers without expenses in range.
