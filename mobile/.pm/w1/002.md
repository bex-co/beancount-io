# Enable lazy tab mounting in (tabs)/\_layout.tsx once tab count grows

`app/(app)/(tabs)/_layout.tsx` sets `lazy: false`, so every tab mounts (and fetches) eagerly on app start. Fine at 4 tabs; with 6 after w1/m2 + w1/m3 it means two extra screens' queries fire before the user visits them.

Flip to lazy mounting (or per-screen `lazy`) and verify no regressions — especially loading-state background colors (dark-mode flicker bit the account picker before). Sub-hour once the new tabs exist.
