## Objective

After an edit or delete, every list that showed the entry reflects the change without an app restart: the Journal tab, Home's Recent Transactions card, and the account-detail journal.

## Context

- Existing pattern: `AddTransactionCallback` in `src/common/globalFnFactory` + `refreshSignal` props on Home cards (`src/screens/home-screen/home-screen.tsx`).
- Journal: `src/screens/journal-screen/`; account detail: `src/screens/account-detail-screen/`.
- Apollo cache: prefer targeted `refetch`/callback over global cache eviction.

## Steps

1. On successful delete/edit, invoke the registered refresh callback (or refetch the affected queries) before navigating back.
2. Verify each surface: Journal list, Home recents, account-detail journal, and the transaction-detail screen itself after an edit.

## Files

- `src/screens/transaction-detail-screen/transaction-detail-screen.tsx`
- `src/screens/edit-transaction-screen/edit-transaction-screen.tsx`

## Acceptance criteria

- [x] Delete an entry → it disappears from Journal, Home recents, and account detail without restart (via Apollo `refetchQueries`).
- [x] Edit an amount → the new amount shows everywhere the entry appears.

## Out of scope

- Offline/optimistic updates.
