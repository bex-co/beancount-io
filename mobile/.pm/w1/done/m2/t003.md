## Objective

Top of the Accounts tab shows the current net worth as a headline plus a line chart of `netWorthData` from the balance sheet, with a time-range selector (existing `TimeRangePills`) that refetches/refilters the series — the Monarch accounts-header experience.

## Context

- Data: `use-balance-sheet` from t001 (`netWorthData: [DateAndBalance]`; balances are `JSONObject` keyed by currency — reuse the balance-parsing helpers in `src/screens/home-screen/selectors/balance-util.ts`).
- Chart: `src/common/d3/line-chart-d3.tsx` (or `interactive-line-chart.tsx` for scrubbing) as used by `src/screens/home-screen/components/account-charts-card.tsx`.
- Range selector: `src/components/time-range-pills/`.
- Loading: `src/components/loading-tile/` with themed background.

## Steps

1. Build `net-worth-header.tsx` under `src/screens/accounts-screen/components/`: headline amount (latest net worth), delta vs range start, line chart.
2. Wire `TimeRangePills` selection to the balance-sheet query's `time` variable (or client-side slicing if the full series is cheap).
3. Add a selector (e.g. `select-net-worth-header.ts`) converting `netWorthData` into chart points; reuse `select-net-worth-series` logic from home where applicable.
4. Handle loading (skeleton with background color) and empty ledger states.
