# Consider folding the Ledger web-view tab into Settings

Once the Accounts (w1/m2) and Reports (w1/m3) tabs land, the tab bar has 6 entries — crowded on a phone (Monarch caps at 5). The Ledger tab is only a WebView of the web editor (`src/screens/ledger-screen/ledger-screen.tsx`), so it could become a row in Settings (or a header affordance) instead of a top-level tab.

Product decision — removing an existing tab should not ride along silently in a milestone. Revisit after w1/m2 ships and the 6-tab layout can be felt in the app.
